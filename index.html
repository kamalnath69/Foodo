Our Story

About Foodo

Born from a simple observation in our college cafeteria, Foodo has grown into a campus-wide movement. We noticed the alarming amount of food being wasted daily while many in our local community struggled with food insecurity.

Our team of passionate students and supportive faculty members work together to:

  • Track and analyze food waste patterns
  • Develop innovative solutions for food conservation
  • Create awareness through workshops and events
Our Mission

What We Do

Our comprehensive approach to tackling food waste through education, action, and innovation.

Education & Awareness

Conducting workshops, seminars, and campaigns to educate students about food waste impact and prevention methods.

Research & Innovation

Analyzing campus food waste patterns and developing innovative solutions for better food management.

Community Action

Organizing food donation drives and implementing waste reduction programs in campus cafeterias.

The Impact

Why Food Waste Matters

Food waste is more than just throwing away leftovers. It's a global crisis with far-reaching implications.

Environmental Impact

Food waste in landfills produces methane, a greenhouse gas 25 times more potent than CO2. Reducing food waste is crucial for fighting climate change.

  • • 3.3 billion tons of CO2 generated annually
  • • 25% of freshwater wasted

Social Impact

While millions struggle with hunger, perfectly good food goes to waste. Addressing food waste is crucial for food security.

  • • 820 million people undernourished globally
  • • 1/3 of food produced could feed all hungry people

Economic Impact

Food waste represents a significant economic loss and inefficiency in our food systems.

  • • $1 trillion economic costs annually
  • • Average family loses $1,600/year on wasted food
